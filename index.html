Upcoming guests

Who is coming up on Bench Tested

More than just guest announcements, this is where the next round of deep-dive conversations starts. Expect talk around electronics, prop design, engineering choices, workflow, screen accuracy, fandom history, and what actually separates solid work from average builds.

Featured guest

Nomake Wan

One of the programmers behind the driving force of GPStar's pack electronics, Nomake Wan helped shape one of the most respected electronic ecosystems in the hobby.

We will get into pack logic, feature design, how electronics choices impact the overall user experience, what makes a system reliable in the field, and the broader philosophy behind building gear that fans actually want to use.
Featured guests

Chris and Andrew, C&A Prop Design

Chris and Andrew from C&A Prop Design will join the show to talk through prop development, fabrication choices, aesthetics, practical problem-solving, and what it takes to balance clean design with real-world functionality.

Expect conversation around build process, materials, finishing, what collectors and builders get wrong, and how design decisions shift when you are making pieces meant to be both impressive and usable.
Featured guest

Ben Eadie

Prop master and curator for Ghostbusters: Afterlife, Ben Eadie brings a film-side perspective that goes beyond fandom and into the real mechanics of how iconic equipment gets brought to life.

We will touch on screen-used prop considerations, storytelling through design, franchise legacy, restoration, curation, and what fans should understand about the difference between movie-ready and convention-ready gear.
